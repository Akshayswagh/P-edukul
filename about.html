About Us

P-eduKUL Digiversity Pvt Ltd

P-eduKUL Digiversity Pvt Ltd is a Maharashtra-based private limited company, established in 2022. Specializing in educational content, P-eduKUL Digiversity is committed to providing high-quality learning materials and resources. As an authorized content partner of ADDA247, P-eduKUL Digiversity plays a crucial role in delivering comprehensive and effective educational content for the integrated program. P-eduKUL is a startup certified company under the Government of India, Ministry of Skills and Entrepreneurship.

Key Services Platform available :

  • Academic delivery
  • Skill Based Education
  • Recruitment Platform
  • Entrepreneurship Incubation Cell

OUR VISION

"To establish a Digital university focusing on Commerce, IT and Management faculty that will strengthen and equip students on employability, entrepreneurship, skill development with research inclination considering economic growth and available opportunities in the country in line with the Industrial requirement thereby enhancing careers of students and thus contributing to Nation building."

OUR MISSION

"We guide and encourage our teenagers in building and maintaining solid relationship with God through teaching of the word of God and unparalleled prayer lifestyle."

Chairman’s Message

Dr. Prashant Ganesh Kulkarni

Chairman, P-eduKUL Digiversity Pvt. Ltd.

“Education is no longer confined to the four walls of a classroom. It is a dynamic journey of discovery, skill, and purpose — and we at P-eduKUL Digiversity are here to guide every learner on that journey.”

At P-eduKUL Digiversity, we are driven by a single vision — to make education relevant, holistic, and future-ready for every student, irrespective of their background, language, or location. In the rapidly evolving landscape of education and employment, we believe that academic knowledge must go hand-in-hand with practical skills, digital fluency, and emotional intelligence.

Our integrated ecosystem of educational services has been thoughtfully curated to empower institutions and learners alike:

  • Composite Skills Labs to nurture STEM abilities, cognitive development, emotional well-being, and hands-on scientific learning in schools.
  • Integrated Learning Programs for competitive exam preparation (CA, CS, JEE, NEET, MPSC, UPSC, SSC, NDA and more) in collaboration with ADDA247, delivered through a robust hybrid model.
  • Skill Development & Vocational Education Courses in fields like Fashion Design, IT, Hospitality, Animation, Logistics, Digital Marketing, and Finance – all designed to prepare students for employment or entrepreneurship.
  • Digital Commerce Education, offering complete subject-wise video learning from Grade XI to M.Com, as well as BBA and MBA programs, available in English and Marathi, to democratize access and support regional language learners.

At the heart of all our offerings is a commitment to quality, innovation, and inclusion. We partner with schools, colleges, and universities to help them transform their campuses into centres of excellence, aligned with the vision of NEP 2020 and the aspirations of a New India.

Whether you're a student seeking success, an educator driving change, or an institution shaping the future — P-eduKUL Digiversity is your trusted ally in this mission.

Together, let us build an ecosystem where learning is joyful, skills are powerful, and education leads to opportunity.
