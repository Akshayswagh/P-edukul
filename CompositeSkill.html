What are Composite Skills Labs?

At P-eduKUL Digiversity, we are proud to introduce Composite Skills Labs — a unique, integrated learning ecosystem designed for 21st-century schools, in alignment with the vision of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.In technical collaboration with India’s leading EdTech platform ADDA247, we offer Hybrid Learning Solutions that blend the best of digital education with on-site academic support, helping institutions deliver career-focused education under one roof.

Our Labs are designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application, focusing on holistic child development across four core domains:

STEM Labs – Science | Technology | Engineering | Mathematics

  • Hands-on learning through experiments, robotics, AI, and coding
  • Real-world challenges that inspire innovation and critical thinking
  • Interactive modules aligned with the NCERT/CBSE/State curriculum
  • Encouraging design thinking and problem-solving abilities

“Don’t just learn science – experience it!”:

Cognition Skills Lab – Sharpening Young Minds

  • Curated tools and games to boost memory, logical reasoning, visual-spatial skills, and executive functions
  • Programs inspired by neuroscience and cognitive science
  • Enhances focus, decision-making, and analytical thinking
  • Regular assessments and adaptive challenges for personalized growth

“Think deeper. Learn smarter.”:


SEL Lab – Social Emotional Learning Lab

  • Fostering empathy, self-awareness, resilience, and positive relationships
  • Role plays, storytelling, group activities, and reflection sessions
  • Mental well-being programs rooted in emotional intelligence
  • Supports the development of confident and compassionate learnerss

“Heart meets mind – because emotional wellness fuels academic success.”

  • Safe and repeatable virtual environments for enhanced learning
  • Aligned with middle and secondary school science curriculum
  • Enables schools to overcome infrastructure limitations and provide access to high-quality practical experiences
  • Safe and repeatable virtual environments for enhanced learning
