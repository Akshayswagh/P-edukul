About Us

P-eduKUL Digiversity Pvt Ltd

P-eduKUL Digiversity Pvt Ltd is a Maharashtra-based private limited company, established in 2022. Specializing in educational content, P-eduKUL Digiversity is committed to providing high-quality learning materials and resources. As an authorized content partner of ADDA247, P-eduKUL Digiversity plays a crucial role in delivering comprehensive and effective educational content for the integrated program. P-eduKUL is a startup certified company under the Government of India, Ministry of Skills and Entrepreneurship.

Key Services Platform available :

  • Academic delivery
  • Skill Based Education
  • Recruitment Platform
  • Entrepreneurship Incubation Cell

Why P-eduKUL ?

Top Reasons to Partner with India’s Most Future-Focused Digital Learning Ecosystem.

NEP 2020 Aligned Educational Solutions

Our programs are fully aligned with the National Education Policy 2020, ensuring that institutions meet national educational reforms with ease — through flexibility, credit-based learning, skill integration, and multilingual content.

AI-Powered, Multilingual Learning Support

Our flagship platform P-eduKUL AI Guru supports education in 55+ Indian regional languages, from Grade 1 to PG level — using Artificial Intelligence to personalize, simplify, and democratize learning and teaching.

Comprehensive Offerings Across Academic and Skill Domains

From Composite Skills Labs and Competitive Exam Prep to Digital Commerce Education and Vocational Skill Development, P-eduKUL offers end-to-end academic and career solutions under one roof.

Hybrid Model: Digital + On-Site Support

We offer a powerful hybrid learning model — combining digitally recorded, high-quality content with on-site training, facilitation, and mentoring for maximum learning impact.

Industry-Ready Curriculum for Employability & Entrepreneurship

Our Skill Development Programs are mapped to NSQF and designed in partnership with industry experts — ensuring learners are job-ready and entrepreneurial from Day 1.

Teacher Empowerment Tools

We offer tools, content, and training to help educators become digitally fluent facilitators — including AI-generated lesson plans, classroom aids, and performance tracking.

Custom Solutions for Every Institution Type

Whether you're a rural school, an urban college, a technical university, or a private coaching institute, we provide customized, scalable, and budget-friendly solutions to meet your exact needs..

Reliable, Scalable, and Future-Ready Infrastructure

Our cloud-based platforms are secure, mobile-friendly, and capable of scaling across thousands of students — backed by real-time analytics and robust content management systems..

Focus on Learning Outcomes & Impact

We go beyond content delivery — we measure and track real-world learning outcomes, student engagement, and academic progress, ensuring measurable institutional success..

Trusted by Educators, Loved by Learners

Our growing community of schools, colleges, universities, and learners across India trust P-eduKUL for our quality, innovation, and unwavering focus on education for the future.

Let’s Build the Future of Education — Together

Whether you want to digitize your curriculum, enhance your teaching, introduce AI tools, or launch employability-driven programs — P-eduKUL is your perfect academic partner.
